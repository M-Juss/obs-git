Process

- Renting requires down payment
    

- Self-Drive requires (Valid ID, Passport, Meralco Bill)

- Owners-Drivers no need ID.

      - 1k additional outside manila

      - Accommodation, Allowance and Food will be catered by the renter.

      - Minimum duration of rent (1-day)

      - Toll Fee will be paid based on consumption.

      - Failed to Return in the said time; Additional Fee based on Hour (may vary based on car rented). 

      - Gas Process

      - Accident

- Human Error - Sagot ng Renter; Technical Error (Sagot ng Owner)

      - Maintenance - Done by them (Every 5km)
